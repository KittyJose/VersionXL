Who and what is VersionXL for?


VersionXL is an Excel versioning control tool that gives teams safety, backup, and allows them to collaborate with confidence.  Teams can work concurrently on the same document and merge it together when they are ready. It brings engineering best practices to Excel while offering visibility into the long-term change history of every file and tracing who made the changes. This is production-grade Excel.

Version Control

Have all the past versions available and be able to roll back to any point of time and prove it to auditors or regulators.

Collaboration

Experiment as you work; if something doesn’t work out, you can always return to an earlier saved state.

Visibility

Get a birds-eye view into all the flows of data in your organization. Run a simple query to see who made a specific change.

Security

Who has sensitive forecast, PII, and GDPR data in their spreadsheets?  Monitor your exposure and enforce compliance & security with VersionXL.

VersionXL helps you effectively manage Excel versioning and collaboration so you can reap the benefits of Excel without problems.
It is a technical solution for non-technical users aimed at solving common problems all from within the Excel interface itself.
This is how it works.

Step 1
Download Excel add-in

Users download the VersionXL add-in for Excel. It then appears in their toolbar providing advanced versioning and collaboration features.

Step 2

Commit your spreadsheet
and share

Commit the initial version of your spreadsheet to VersionXL
Assign the users you want to work with it
They ‘pull’ it to their own device to work from, with full access to the version history
When done they ‘push’ it back to the central repository and merge it back in

Step 3
Central control and visibility

From the VersionXL dashboard:
See all of the spreadsheets within your organization
See all versions, changes, and access details.
Query changes and version differences
